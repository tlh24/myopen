Project: stage4, http://code.google.com/p/myopen/

Layer Stack: 
1 - Top, top of board.  				stage4-Top.pho
2 - L1, inner layer, ground. 			stage4-Inner_L1.pho
3 - L2, inner layer, power/routing. 		stage4-Inner_L2.pho
4 - L3, inner layer, power/routing. 		stage4-Inner_L3.pho
5 - L4, inner layer, ground. 			stage4-Inner_L4.pho
6 - Bot, bottom of board.   			stage4-Bot.pho

Note: all layers are viewed from the TOP of the board. 

* PCB to be finished to 0.047" +- 0.006" thickness (1.2mm) 
* PCB to be manufactured in accordance with IPC-6011 and IPC-6012 class 2. 
* PCB to be constructed of FR4, high temperature option not required. 
* Routing outline in stage4-Drawings.pho
* Minimum trace/space 0.005" 
* Minimim via drill 0.006"
* Drill file: stage4.drl
* Surface finish to be solder mask over bare copper (SMBOC), green or blue, 
	in stage4-Mask_Top.pho and stage4-Mask_Bot.pho, both sides. 
	with 150 micro inches Nickel (Ni)
	followed by immersion gold. 
* Solder mask may be enlarged as needed. 
* Silkcreen (stage4-Silkscreen_Top.pho) to be white or yellow, only top of board
