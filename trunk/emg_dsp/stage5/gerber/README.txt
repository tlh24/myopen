Project: stage5, http://code.google.com/p/myopen/

Layer Stack: 
1 - Front, top of board.  				stage5-Front.gtl
2 - L1, inner layer, ground. 			stage5-Inner2.gbr
3 - L2, inner layer, power/routing. 		stage5-Inner3.gbr
4 - L3, inner layer, power/routing. 		stage5-Inner4.gbr
5 - L4, inner layer, ground. 			stage5-Inner5.gbr
6 - Back, bottom of board.   			stage5-Back.gbl

Note: all layers are viewed from the TOP of the board. 

* PCB to be finished to 0.047" +- 0.006" thickness (1.2mm) 
* PCB to be manufactured in accordance with IPC-6011 and IPC-6012 class 2. 
* PCB to be constructed of FR4, high temperature option required, Tg >= 170C
* Routing outline in stage5-Drawings.gbr
* Minimum trace/space 0.005" / 0.0045"
* Minimim via drill 0.008"
* Drill file: stage5.drl
* All holes to be plated through.
* Surface finish to be solder mask over bare copper (SMBOC), blue, 
	in stage5-Mask_Front.gts and stage5-Mask_Back.gbs, both sides. 
	with 150 micro inches Nickel (Ni)
	followed by immersion gold. 
* Solder mask may be enlarged as needed. 
* Silkcreen (stage5-SilkS_Front.gto , stage5-SilkS_Back.gbo ) to be white or yellow, only top of board
